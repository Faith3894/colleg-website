About Us

Annex Computer and Business College was founded in th year 2002. The college has since then aimed to providing quality training aimed at meeting the changing demands of motdern business and ICT.

Our Mission

To offer quality and affordable training in ICT, Accountd & Business skilss and competence to enable them serve competently locally and beyond.

Our Vision

To offer improved access to tertially training by equiping our students to play an active role in the global knowledge and build capacity within their own institutions and places of work.

Our Motto

Quality and Profesionalism.
